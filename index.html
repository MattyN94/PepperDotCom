A short story about a dog.

Let's start by introducing you to Pepsi (I call her Pepsi more than I call her Pepper). She's two and a half years old, and she's a smooth coat Cockapoo. Yes, she doesn't look like one.

Her father is a working cocker and her mother is a Cockapoo so technically, Peps is 75% cocker spaniel and 25% poodle.

Because of this, she does actually shed her fur even though most of us get poodle-cross breeds to stop this exact thing from happening. Pepsi's got the incredible nose of a pedigree working cocker, so she can smell a sandwich from a mile away and will sprint at full speed to go an eat it off the floor. As hinted at earlier, you'd be forgiven for thinking she's just a cocker spaniel, until you notice her snout is less boxy than a spaniels.
